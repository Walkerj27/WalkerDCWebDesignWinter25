My Favorite Hobby is Fishing

I fished for the first time when I was eight with my dad, my grandpa, and one of my grandpa's friends. We went out early in the morning and caught a lot of fish which was way more than we had expected. After this outing, we had started to do this every year. Eventually, I started fishing in the ponds nearby with my friends and we would ride are bikes their and stay for hours. After this we never stopped and would go very often in the summer.

How I got Started

Usually when I go fishing, I will fish for largemouth bass, catfish, or carp. For ponds, these three species are quite common and they are the most fun to fish for. They can be found in many different parts of Michigan and other states too. There are many other kinds of fish in the Great Lakes like musky, sturgeon, lake trout, and many other kinds of bass. These are the best fish to go for as they usually will be the most fun to catch and the biggest.

Fish Species

Like stated earlier, largemouth bass, carp, and catfish will be the most common fish to try and catch. Along with these three, bluegill can be common too for new fishermen to help them learn to fish. Firstly, largemouth bass will ahve the most variety. They will bite a lot of different rigs, but it just depends on the conditions for that day. For example, if the water is muddy and it is a windy day then white lures will be the best as the will shine in the murky water.
