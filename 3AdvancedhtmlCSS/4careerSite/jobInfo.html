Data Scientist

Description

A data scientist is someone who uses statistical data to make important insights and ideas that could help the company to grow. Firstly, they have to collect and organize the data that they need for their certain topic. Next, a data scientist will need to make conclusions based on the data sets looked at. Finally, they will find solutions for the problem and they will help make improvements based on their analysis.

Average Salary

The average salary for a data scientist is between $100,000-$120,000.

Employers

  • Healthcare
  • Retail/Marketing
  • Entertainment
