Overview

IISE Annual Conference & Expo 2025 will take place from May 31 to June 3 in Atlanta, Georgia. The event is a meeting of industrial and systems engineering practitioners, academicians, and students who exchange innovations, network, and seek solutions in fields like supply chain, healthcare, and ergonomics.

Introduction

Discover the future of industrial and systems engineering at the IISE Annual Conference & Expo 2025 in Atlanta, Georgia. This event brings professionals, researchers, and students together for four days of deliberative sessions, experiential learning, and impactful collaboration from a wide range of industries.
