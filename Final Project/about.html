Mission

The IISE Conference aims to encourage innovation, collaboration, and exchange of knowledge among professionals, researchers, and students involved in the industrial and systems engineering field. The conference is a platform for encouraging the profession through the exchange of cutting-edge research, best practices, and emerging technologies.

Vision

To be the world's premier international forum that drives the future of industrial and systems engineering by convening global thought leaders, fostering innovation, and enabling a more efficient, sustainable, and data-based world.

Goals

  • Promote academic and professional excellence in industrial and systems engineering.
  • Highlight advancements in methodologies, tools, and technologies.
  • Support the development of the next generation of engineers and leaders.
